Caramelized Baked Chicken

Description

Roast chicken pieces with sticky, sweet and tangy coating.
  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Step 2 Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Mix together the oil, soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, salt and pepper. Pour over the chicken.
  3. Step 3 Bake in preheated oven for one hour, or until sauce is caramelized.
